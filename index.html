Special Recipes

Taste of Precious

Experience the royal flavors of Uttar Pradesh with our fragrant vegetarian biryani

a timeless dish rooted in Nawabi heritage. Cooked in the traditional dum style, this biryani is a symphony of basmati rice, fresh seasonal vegetables, and aromatic spices like saffron, cardamom, and cloves. Each layer is slow-cooked to perfection, allowing the rich flavors to meld beautifully. Served with raita and tangy salan, it's a wholesome and hearty dish that celebrates the elegance of Awadhi cuisine — all without a trace of meat.
